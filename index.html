About Us
UAE-Based Peer-to-Peer Digital Asset Exchange

B3OTC is a UAE-based peer-to-peer digital asset exchange specializing in high-volume AED ⇄ USDT transactions. Established in 2021, we have built a strong presence in the market by delivering reliable, fast, and flexible liquidity solutions. With a monthly turnover exceeding $100 million USDT, B3OTC operates as a trusted bridge between traditional finance and digital assets, serving individuals, traders, and businesses that require efficient execution at scale.

We provide seamless conversion between AED and USDT through multiple settlement methods:

Bank Transfers
Cash Transactions
Manager Cheque Settlements

Our infrastructure is designed for real-world execution — enabling clients to move between fiat and digital assets with speed, discretion, and operational clarity.

B3OTC actively operates across leading digital asset platforms and ecosystems, including:

Binance (Official Partner since 2021)
Bybit
Other major global exchanges

This multi-platform presence allows us to access deep liquidity and deliver consistent pricing and execution for our clients.

At B3OTC, we focus on:

High-volume transaction capability
Flexible settlement solutions
Secure and structured workflows
Strong operational discipline
We are built for serious volume — not retail limitations. B3OTC is more than an exchange; it is a liquidity partner supporting the growth of the digital asset economy in the region.
